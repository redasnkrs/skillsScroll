# Solo Honor Mode Stealth Archer

## Overview

A solo, Honor Mode Dark Urge Stealth Archer route focused on hyper-consistent advantage, burst damage from Titanstring + Strength stacking, action economy via Bloodlust, and long-rest minimization. Uses stealth/invisibility, speed, and targeted shopping to secure core power spikes early.

Core multiclass: Ranger (Gloomstalker) 5 / Rogue (Assassin) 4 / Fighter 2 / War Cleric 1.

Mods used in the video are QoL only; the build works in unmodded.

---

## Quick Start — Stats, Feats, Spells

- Race: Human (video run). Any works; consistency races include Halfling, Gnome, Duergar, Githyanki.
    
- Background: Any; stealth-proficient backgrounds helpful.
    
- Starting stats (video): DEX 17, WIS 16, CON 14, STR 8–10, CHA 8, INT dump.
    
- Early spells to prep/use:
    
    - Ranger: Longstrider (no concentration), Enhance Leap (setup), Pass Without Trace (Act 1–2 stealth), Archery fighting style.
        
- Key Feats/ASI:
    
    - Level 4 (Ranger): Sharpshooter.
        
    - Level 8 (Rogue ASI): +2 DEX (to 19 or 20 depending on items).
        
    - Remaining: no more feats needed—item synergies + Sharpshooter carry.

## Act 1 Route (power set-up, Titanstring, economy, skip to Act 2 early)

### Nautiloid & Beach Setup

- Strip shoes/clothes for movement; grab camp clothes & early potions.
- Loot for Speed potions; organize hotbar.
- Take Lae’zel’s armor for early survivability, but remember it imposes stealth disadvantage; swap out once stealth checks matter.

### Grove, Mask, Speed Tech

- Grab Shapeshifting Mask (to disguise), Strength Elixir, Feather Fall, Speed potions (two under rock with STR Elixir + shovel).
- Torch + Feather Fall mobility; use Longstrider + Enhance Leap for traversal.

### Space Gummy (Illithid Tadpole) & Raphael trigger

- Pick up “space gummy” at the pod encounter to start tadpole powers and later Reverberation synergies.

### Volothamp (Volo) camp economy trick

- Invite Volo to camp; daily pickpocket for gold before partial long rests to fund key shopping.

### Goblin Village & Priestess Gut

- Use disguise to pass goblin checks; loot windmill chest for Alchemist’s Fire x2. Kill drunk guard for XP safely from roof.
- Do Gut’s drink-and-dream scene (human can drink), get Dark Urge boons; later kill Gut.

### Titanstring Bow quest

- Clear the goblin side targets; buy Gloves of Archery from trader (if funds permit).
- Acquire Titanstring Bow in the Brim area (best-in-class bow due to STR mod double-dip with special arrows when buffed).

### Early Act 2 Moonrise skip (Moonlantern) — WARNING: precise path

- Apply Longstrider + Enhance Leap + Feather Fall; use torch pathing to reach Moonrise at low level. Follow Morgana’s left path to avoid ambush; jump chain through curse ticks safely.
- Enter Moonrise and grab Moonlantern; return to Act 1 for continued setup.

### Act 2 Shopping Trip (mid-Act 1 timing)

- Buy Risky Ring (advantage on attacks, disadvantage on saves) and Ring of Free Action (ignore difficult terrain, immune to restrain/paralyze) at Araj in Moonrise. These are run-defining.
- Invest 600 gold with Talli to unlock her inventory scaling; buy Shadeclinger Armor (grants “Clinging Shadows” advantage on saves while obscured; the buff persists after swapping armor until long rest), Holy Water, Smoke Powder bombs, Bloodlust Elixir(s).

### Clinging Shadows trick (keep advantage on saves)

- Equip Shadeclinger briefly in darkness to gain Clinging Shadows; swap back to your preferred armor—the advantage buff persists until long rest.

### Underdark & Grymforge

- Underdark entry via ruined temple; pass dialogues for safe entry.
- Mushroom run (Tongue of Madness, Timask Spores) for crafting haste/speed potions.
- Club of Hill Giant Strength (19 STR) from smashing the chair in the Sussur tower; pair later with Bloodlust Elixir + Titanstring for massive damage.
- Grym boss: throw bludgeoning junk (shoes, boxes) while he’s heated for double bludgeoning vulnerability; last-hit with Titanstring for XP spike.

### Misc. Act 1 targets

- Headband of Intellect (for dialogue checks like Githyanki Inquisitor machine).
- Pass Without Trace and Assassination loops to clear pockets of mobs safely.
- Hag fight: ONLY engage with Ring of Free Action equipped (prevents Hold/Restrain cheese). Push her below 30% to force retreat; take the hair.

## Act 2 Route (Moonrise clear, Ketheric, Pixie Blessing, key rings/boots)

### Moonrise murder-clear & plate armor sale

- Stealth-clear Moonrise for XP and gold; loot plate armors to sell to Talli for best margins (approval helps).

### Prison, Pixie Lantern blessing

- Clear prison while avoiding bridge one-shots; free the pixie to bless your lantern (no shadow curse damage while lit; reduces Act 2 traversal risk).

### Must-buy arrows & consumables

- Arrows of Many Targets (priority whenever seen), Darkness arrows, Elemental arrows, Oil of Accuracy, Smoke Powder bombs, Holy Water. Keep inventory flush for bosses.

### Boots of Stormy Clamor (Reverberation engine)

- From Duergar merchant: “Boots … whenever you inflict a condition, apply 2 turns of Reverberation.” Synergizes with tadpole’s Ability Drain on attack to stack Reverberation reliably for thunder damage + prone at 5 stacks. Keep equipped all game.

### Tadpole Powers

- Ability Drain (reduces target DEX by 1 on attack) to count as condition -> fuels Reverberation. Later Cold Awakened (explosive last-hits) as threshold permits.

### Ketheric (rooftop) and Myrkul

- Get rooftop intimidation letter to lower DC (optional).
- Boss handling: position for Dread Ambusher alpha, use special arrows and Bloodlust chain kills; brand vulnerabilities with amulet (per long rest) if needed.

## Gear & Consumables — What to Buy/Use and Why

### Early/Mid
- Titanstring Bow: STR-mod damage double-dips with special arrows when buffed (Magic Weapon, Elemental Weapon), best sustained DPS bow.
- Club of Hill Giant Strength: Sets STR to 19; combo with Bloodlust and Titanstring.
- Gloves of Archery: +2 ranged attack bonus; stable accuracy early.
- Risky Ring: Advantage on attacks; damage consistency outweighs save disadvantage (mitigated by Clinging Shadows).
- Ring of Free Action: Immunity to restrain/paralyze + ignore difficult terrain—mandatory for Hag, many control-heavy fights.
- Shadeclinger Armor (buff): Equip in darkness to gain “Clinging Shadows” advantage on saves; persists until long rest even after swapping.
- Boots of Stormy Clamor: Apply Reverberation when you inflict a condition; pair with Tadpole Ability Drain to stack and trigger thunder/prone. Keep equipped permanently.
- Headband of Intellect: Equip for specific high-DC dialogues (Gith machine).
- Smuggler’s Ring (optional stealth): +2 Stealth/Sleight of Hand; minor downside.

### Endgame
- Craterflesh Gloves (DRS): Big damage routing; swap in for endgame burst sequence.
- Helldusk Boots: Defensive/offensive utility for late bosses.
- Deathstalker Mantle (Dark Urge): On kill, invis for two turns—strong but be cautious: combat can end if enemies “Detect Presence” at your past location. Use intentionally.

### Consumables (keep stocked)
- Bloodlust Elixir: Extra action on kill—chain kills.
- Speed/Haste potions: Mobility & extra attacks for alpha clears. Craft from mushrooms; buy when seen.
- Oil of Accuracy: Temporary accuracy boost for Sharpshooter shots.
- Arrows of Many Targets: Priority purchase—multi-hit burst & add-on effects.
- Elemental/Darkness/Special arrows: Openers and control.
- Smoke Powder bombs + barrels: Boss skips and environmental kills.

### Buff Scrolls/Spells
- Magic Weapon / Elemental Weapon on Titanstring (via Conduit ring synergy when concentrating; Elemental adds +1 attack and 1–4 thunder).
- Shield of Faith (AC boost), Pass Without Trace (stealth +10), Longstrider (passive speed).

## Combat Pattern — How to Execute

1. Prebuff: Longstrider, Pass Without Trace (if stealth), Magic/Elemental Weapon on Titanstring, Oil of Accuracy, Bloodlust Elixir.
2. Open: Dread Ambusher shot from stealth; use special arrows for double-dip damage; Risky Ring ensures advantage.
3. Chain: On kill, Bloodlust grants extra action; Fighter Action Surge for another sequence; War Priest charges for bonus attacks when needed.
4. Control: Ring of Free Action prevents shutdown; Boots + Ability Drain stack Reverberation passively.
5. Reset: Bonus Action Hide (Gloomstalker) or invis (consumable/cloak) to break line-of-sight; re-open next target.

Notes:
- Always prioritize threats that can end solo runs (Paladins, Hold Person casters, Fear casters).
- For bosses like Grym: exploit vulnerabilities (bludgeoning, heat state).
- For Hag: free action ring required; aim for <30% retreat threshold.
- For brain stem: kill dragon first turn to avoid wipe conditions.
